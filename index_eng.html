WTF? WTH! What’s this?

What the Hack 2014 —
is a 44-hour hackathon for developers
that will be held in Belarus for the first time
within the scope of european code week (#codeEU)

The main idea and goal

Within 44 hours the participants should create a prototype
and/or a demo that will impress the jury and the audience.

What can you expect from What the Hack?

  • An opportunity to experiment with new technologies.
  • Master classes from leading Belarusian and European developers.
  • A great chance to get advice from mentors of international level.
  • Assistance in learning almost any known technology.
  • A real chance to realize some idea that has always been postponed due to the lack of time.
  • Estimation of the result of your efforts and a possibility to win prizes from the organizers and the coolest Belarusian communities.
  • Lots of fun, useful contacts and valuable knowledge.

You WON'T meet at What the Hack:

You WON'T meet
  • hiring,
  • advertising,
  • showing off,
  • brainwashing,
  • boring lectures,
  • dodgy people.

We are open to

  • Any ideas, from useful to ridiculous and mad ones.
  • The use of new technologies and approaches.
  • Innovative approaches to the implementation of standard solutions.
  • “Quick and dirty” prototypes.
  • Beautiful demos even if they run in one browser or in one OS.
  • Tests for the sake of tests, experiments for experiments.
  • Just attempts to do something for self-development or entertainment.

Who can participate?

  • Web-developers.
  • Mobile app developers.
  • Other developers - of any level of expertise, specialization, gender, age and views.

We DON'T expect at hackathon:

We DON'T expect:
  • HR (sorry, friends).
  • Inadequate people, pessimists, die-hards.
  • "Conceptual startup creators" (i.e., people
    who are only able to generate crazy ideas
    and are constantly looking for programmers
    to implement this ideas)    .

What is so special about What the Hack?

Unlike many Hackathons held in Minsk What the Hack
is not aimed at creating a ready startup or a complete
prototype of the idea that will save the whole world.
(But we do not mind if you save the world :)

What The Hack 2014 is a hackathon from and for developers.

The main goal is to acquire new knowledge and skills, meet interesting people,
invent some cool things, spend time usefully and eat free cookies :)
